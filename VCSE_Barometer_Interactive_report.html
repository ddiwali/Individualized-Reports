VCSE Barometer Survey Wave 6 Overview, April 2024

Wave 6 of the VCSE Barometer was conducted between 10 April and 1 May 2024 and comprises the views of 10 VCSE organisations completing at least 90% of the survey.

This bar chart shows the top concerns affecting organisations in April 2024. The data shows that the top three concerns faced by the organisations are Income, Volunteer Recruitment, and Increasing demand.

Nottingham Trent University VCSE Observatory, VCSE Barometer Survey

The findings in this report are based on an analysis of Wave 6 of the VCSE Barometer, a quarterly survey of UK voluntary, community, and social enterprise sector organisations designed and administered by Nottingham Trent University's National VCSE Data and Insights Observatory. The VCSE Barometer offers a snapshot into real-time trends, changes, and challenges in the VCSE sector. It is supported by over 50 major social sector membership organisations and networks, and findings are shared every quarter with national policy and decision-makers, local infrastructure organisations, and the VCSE sector.
